About the Driver Collector

Welcome to the Driver Collector app — a space dedicated to showcasing today’s top Formula 1 drivers. From world champions to rising stars, you’ll find profiles, stats, and fun facts all in one place.

This project was built to practice Django development while celebrating the excitement of Formula 1 racing. Helmet on, lights out, and let’s go racing!
